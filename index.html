About Potassium

Potassium:
Potassium is an electrolyte, or mineral, found in various foods and is available as a dietary supplement. This is present in all body tissues and is required for normal cell function such as proper nerve transmission, muscle contraction and kidney function. Since most people do not ingest enough potassium in their diets, we recommend increasing consumption of fruits and vegetables since these foods have the highest density of potassium.
